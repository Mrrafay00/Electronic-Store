Wearable-Technology

Wearable technology consists of electronic devices worn on the body, like fitness trackers and smartwatches. These gadgets monitor health metrics (like heart rate and activity levels), connect to smartphones for notifications, and often feature GPS for navigation. They help users track fitness goals, monitor health conditions, and enhance daily life through data analysis and integration with apps. Examples include Fitbit, Apple Watch, and smart glasses. Overall, wearable tech improves health management and personal convenience.
